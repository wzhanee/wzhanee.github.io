Dr. ZHAN Wen (詹文)

Assistant Professor
School of Electronics and Communication Engineering,
Sun Yat-sen University, China
Email: zhanw6@mail.sysu.edu.cn

Dr. Zhan Wen received his B.S. degree and M.S. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China, in 2012 and 2015, respectively (Supervisor: Prof. Xuesong Tan). He  received  his PhD degree  from City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China, in 2019 (Supervisor: Prof. Lin Dai). He was a Research Assistant and then a Part-Time Postdoc at City University of Hong Kong. Since 2020, He has been with the School of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, China, where he is currently an Assistant Professor. His research interests include Internet of things, modeling and performance optimization of next-generation mobile communication systems, machine learning and its application in wireless communications.
